## 8. Rooms Section Direction

The room section should feel premium, warm, and story-driven. Each room should have its own identity, not just a price card.

Design style:

* Large rounded image cards
* Ivory background
* Lime price tags / CTA highlights
* Room names in elegant serif font
* Room details in clean modern sans-serif
* Each room card should include: image, room name, short description, price, amenities, and “Book on WhatsApp” button

Room name font:

* Cormorant Garamond / Playfair Display for room names
* Manrope / Plus Jakarta Sans for all other text
